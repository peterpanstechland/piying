# Asset Creation Guide

This guide provides detailed specifications for creating all required assets for the Shadow Puppet Interactive System.

## Overview

The system requires:
- 3 base videos (30 seconds each) for scenes A, B, and C
- 3 scene icon images
- 10 guidance images (3 for Scene A, 3 for Scene B, 4 for Scene C)

## Base Videos

### Technical Specifications

**Required Format:**
- Duration: Exactly 30 seconds
- Resolution: 1920x1080 (Full HD) or 1280x720 (HD)
- Frame Rate: 30 FPS
- Codec: H.264
- Container: MP4
- Bitrate: 5-10 Mbps (for good quality)
- Audio: Optional (can be silent or include ambient music)

### Scene A: Martial Arts Performance (sceneA_base.mp4)

**Theme:** Traditional Chinese martial arts stage

**Visual Elements:**
- Background: Red and gold traditional Chinese theater backdrop
- Elements: Decorative patterns, lanterns, or traditional stage curtains
- Color Palette: Red (#DC143C), Gold (#FFD700), Black (#000000)
- Atmosphere: Energetic, powerful, traditional

**Suggested Content:**
- Static shot of a traditional Chinese stage
- Slow panning across decorative elements
- Subtle animated elements (floating particles, gentle fabric movement)
- Traditional Chinese patterns (clouds, dragons, phoenixes)

**Timing Breakdown:**
- 0-8s: Left side of stage (character enters from left)
- 8-18s: Center stage (main performance area)
- 18-30s: Right side of stage (character exits right)

### Scene B: Dance Performance (sceneB_base.mp4)

**Theme:** Elegant performance stage

**Visual Elements:**
- Background: Soft gradient stage with spotlight effects
- Elements: Flowing curtains, elegant patterns
- Color Palette: Purple (#9370DB), Pink (#FFB6C1), White (#FFFFFF)
- Atmosphere: Graceful, flowing, artistic

**Suggested Content:**
- Elegant stage with soft lighting
- Gentle color transitions
- Flowing abstract patterns
- Spotlight effects that create depth

**Timing Breakdown:**
- 0-10s: Upper stage area (character enters from above)
- 10-25s: Center stage (main performance area)
- 25-30s: Lower stage area (character exits downward)

### Scene C: Story Performance (sceneC_base.mp4)

**Theme:** Narrative storytelling environment

**Visual Elements:**
- Background: Storybook-style scenery
- Elements: Trees, buildings, or landscape silhouettes
- Color Palette: Blue (#4169E1), Orange (#FFA500), Brown (#8B4513)
- Atmosphere: Narrative, journey-like, expressive

**Suggested Content:**
- Landscape or cityscape silhouette
- Day-to-night transition or scene changes
- Environmental elements (trees, buildings, mountains)
- Subtle movement (clouds, leaves, ambient elements)

**Timing Breakdown:**
- 0-6s: Right side of scene (character enters from right)
- 6-18s: Walking across scene (left movement)
- 18-26s: Left side of scene (story climax)
- 26-30s: Far left (character exits)

### Video Creation Tools

**Recommended Software:**
- Adobe After Effects (professional)
- Blender (free, 3D animation)
- DaVinci Resolve (free, video editing)
- Canva (simple, web-based)

**Quick Creation Method:**
1. Create static background image (1920x1080)
2. Import into video editor
3. Add subtle animations (zoom, pan, particles)
4. Export as 30-second MP4 at 30 FPS

## Scene Icons

### Technical Specifications

**Required Format:**
- Size: 256x256 pixels
- Format: PNG with transparency
- Color Depth: 32-bit (RGBA)
- File Size: < 100 KB

### Scene A Icon (sceneA_icon.png)

**Design:**
- Central element: Martial arts figure in action pose
- Style: Silhouette or simplified line art
- Colors: Black silhouette with red/gold accents
- Background: Transparent

**Suggested Elements:**
- Kung fu stance figure
- Fist or kick motion
- Traditional martial arts symbols
- Dynamic pose showing movement

### Scene B Icon (sceneB_icon.png)

**Design:**
- Central element: Dancing figure in graceful pose
- Style: Flowing, elegant silhouette
- Colors: Purple/pink gradient or solid silhouette
- Background: Transparent

**Suggested Elements:**
- Ballet or contemporary dance pose
- Flowing dress or fabric
- Arms extended gracefully
- Sense of movement and elegance

### Scene C Icon (sceneC_icon.png)

**Design:**
- Central element: Storytelling figure or book symbol
- Style: Narrative, expressive silhouette
- Colors: Blue/orange or neutral tones
- Background: Transparent

**Suggested Elements:**
- Figure with expressive gesture
- Open book or scroll
- Speech or thought bubble
- Journey or path symbol

### Icon Creation Tools

**Recommended Software:**
- Adobe Illustrator (professional)
- Inkscape (free, vector graphics)
- Figma (free, web-based)
- GIMP (free, raster graphics)

**Quick Creation Method:**
1. Find or create silhouette of desired pose
2. Apply appropriate colors
3. Add simple decorative elements
4. Export as PNG with transparency at 256x256

## Guidance Images

### Technical Specifications

**Required Format:**
- Size: 800x600 pixels (minimum)
- Format: PNG or JPG
- Color Depth: 24-bit RGB
- File Size: < 500 KB
- Style: Clear, easy to understand

### Design Principles

**Visual Style:**
- Stick figures or simplified human forms
- Clear pose indication
- Minimal background
- High contrast for visibility

**Content Requirements:**
- Show the desired pose or action
- Use arrows to indicate movement direction
- Include key body positions (arms, legs, torso)
- Simple and unambiguous

## Testing Assets

After creating assets, verify:

1. **Videos:**
   - Play in VLC or similar player
   - Check duration is exactly 30 seconds
   - Verify resolution and frame rate
   - Ensure file size is reasonable (< 50 MB)

2. **Icons:**
   - Open in image viewer
   - Check transparency works correctly
   - Verify size is 256x256 pixels
   - Ensure clarity at display size

3. **Guidance Images:**
   - Open in image viewer
   - Check poses are clear and understandable
   - Verify size is adequate (800x600+)
   - Ensure good contrast and visibility
